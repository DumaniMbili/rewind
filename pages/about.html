Cristelle Barbeau embodies a captivating mix of confidence, optimism, and a relentless passion for life. With an unwavering desire to uplift and support others, she actively contributes to Dream Big Stay Humble, a non-profit organization focused on empowering today's youth. Driven by determination and a goal-oriented mindset, Cristelle fearlessly embraces challenges, seeing them as opportunities for growth. Failure, to her, is a mere stepping stone to learn from and improve upon.

This resilient mindset permeates her business, "Criskiddo Designs." Creativity flows effortlessly through Cristelle's veins, as she finds inspiration in every corner of her world. Thinking outside the box is second nature to her, complemented by her meticulous attention to detail. Cristelle's deep appreciation for art has earned her accolades, such as the prestigious Ludrek Floating Trophy for Best Visual Arts Practical Learner during her high school years.

Her dedication to the craft led her to pursue further studies in Fine Art and Design, where she secured top honors in drawing and design. Cristelle's vibrant spirit, unwavering determination, and undeniable talent continue to leave an indelible mark on the realms of creativity and philanthropy, illuminating the path she takes.

Rewind was born from a compelling vision: to curate a captivating experience for young adults in Durban. Our aim is to provide an avenue for exploration of new restaurants while fostering skill development, savoring live music by local artists, and creating lasting memories.

Most importantly, Rewind serves as a platform for networking with diverse individuals from various walks of life. We redefine the concept of a typical Sip & Paint event by offering a premium experience at an accessible middle-class price point. While our brand initially revolved around Sip & Paint events, we are driven by a broader mission to uplift and showcase emerging artists.

Rewind aims to provide these talented individuals with the professional platform they deserve, enabling them to showcase their work to a local audience and establish valuable connections within the art world. Over the course of our journey, we have already achieved notable success. Two of our Sip & Paint events were resounding hits at renowned venues such as Grimaldis Umhlanga and Madam & Sir on Florida.

Furthermore, we organized exceptional jazz and poetry nights at Jazzy Rainbow and Lucky's Bar in Windermere before their unfortunate closure due to the COVID-19 pandemic. At Rewind, our target is to continue captivating and engaging our audience with a range of events and experiences. We are dedicated to creating a vibrant atmosphere where young adults can immerse themselves in art, music, and culinary delights.

By doing so, we aspire to foster connections, inspire creativity, and contribute to the growth and development of Durban's cultural landscape.
